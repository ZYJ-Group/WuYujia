2.论文
摘要：近年来，基于深度神经网络的三维数据生成问题引起了显著的研究兴趣。尽管生成对抗网络（GANs）在图像生成任务中取得了巨大成功，但它们在三维点云生成领域的应用仍然有限。本文提出了一种名为TreeGAN的新型GAN，用于无监督地从随机潜在代码生成三维点云。TreeGAN的核心是树状结构图卷积网络（TreeGCN），该网络利用祖先信息增强了特征的表示能力。实验结果表明，TreeGAN在多个类别的点云补全中，在传统的评估指标上优于现有的GAN方法，点云补全效果较为明显。
In recent years, the generation of three-dimensional data based on deep neural networks has garnered considerable research interest. Despite the significant success of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) in image generation tasks, their application in the field of three-dimensional point cloud generation remains limited. This paper introduces a novel GAN named TreeGAN, designed for the unsupervised generation of three-dimensional point clouds from random latent codes. The essence of TreeGAN lies in the tree-structured graph convolutional network (TreeGCN), which leverages ancestral information to enhance the representational power of features. Experimental results demonstrate that TreeGAN outperforms existing GAN methods in traditional evaluation metrics for point cloud completion across various categories, with notably improved completion effects.
